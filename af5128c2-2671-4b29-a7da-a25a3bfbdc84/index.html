About

Prof. Dana T. Redford is Funder and President of the Portugal Entrepreneurship Education Platform and an internationally-recognized expert on entrepreneurship and public policy. He has worked with the US Department of Commerce, the European Commission, the OECD, the UN and various European and African governments, and is on the Advisory Board of the Institute of European Studies.

He teaches at the Universidade Católica Portuguesa, Porto, and was previously Faculty Coordinator and Guest Lecturer at the Haas School of BusinessCenter for Executive Education at UC Berkeley, where did his post-doc research. Redford holds a BA (Hons.) in Political Science from University of Colorado at Boulder and a PhD in Management from ISCTE-Lisbon.
